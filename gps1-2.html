What is pair programming?

Pair programming is when two people program together. It's a way for people to put their heads together on a project. This is useful because no two people have the same expertise and the same knowledge, and pair programming enables programmers to pick other programmers' brains. It also enables people to teach each other new things; pair programming is as much a teaching experience as it is a learning experience.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

Pair programming is important to DBC because in the workplace, developers pair program often. Since the ability to pair program will be expected of developers, DBC wants us to get experience pair programming so that we are good at it by the time we enter the workforce.

Compare Driver and Navigator

The driver codes and the navigator guides the driver and does the research. The navigator ensures that the code is both accurate and efficient, and catches any mistakes that may arise.
